Nanhu Interactive was built on a simple observation: US small businesses were being charged $5,000–$15,000 for websites that a skilled team could build in a week for a fraction of the price. We decided to change that.

What We Actually Do

We design and build high-converting websites for US small businesses — dental clinics, law firms, real estate agents, plumbers, electricians, HVAC companies, and cleaners. Every site is custom designed, mobile-first, and built specifically for your industry.

We also build AI voice receptionists that answer your business phone 24/7 — booking appointments, answering FAQs, and capturing leads even when you're busy on the job.

Our clients are service businesses that want to look professional, rank on Google, and stop losing customers to competitors with better websites. We make that happen in 7 days or less.
