Our Story

Rooted in tradition, made for today.

We started Nonna's with one goal: to bring exceptionally crafted, naturally leavened pizza to the neighborhood without the white-tablecloth attitude.

Our dough ferments for 72 hours. Our tomatoes come straight from Campania. But our playlists are loud, our natural wines are funky, and our doors are open to everyone.
