A Paradigm Shift in Medicine

The traditional healthcare system is designed for volume, not value. We have completely restructured the clinic model to put you back at the center of your care.

Standard Primary Care

  • 15-Minute Visits: Rushed appointments focused on treating symptoms rather than root causes.

  • Weeks to be Seen: Long wait times to schedule appointments when you're actually sick.

  • Gatekeepers: Navigating receptionists and automated phone trees to reach your doctor.

The Vita Experience

  • 60-Minute Visits: Deep, comprehensive evaluations focusing on longevity and preventative health.

  • Same-Day Appointments: Guaranteed same-day or next-day visits when urgent needs arise.

  • Direct Access: Your physician's personal cell phone number for texts, calls, and telehealth.
